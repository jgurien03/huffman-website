Huffman Text Compression:
Huffman compression is a widely-used technique for reducing the size of data files. It works by assigning shorter codes to frequently occurring symbols and longer codes to less common symbols. This efficient encoding allows for effective compression and decompression of data while maintaining its integrity. Huffman compression finds applications in file compression formats, multimedia codecs, and network protocols, enabling efficient storage and transmission of digital information.
How to Run:
To run, simply input a file into the 'insert file button.' Then you can either choose to compress or decompress your file. Pressing the 'compress' button will download your huffman encoded file. To decode the file, simply insert the encoded file, press the 'decompress' button, and wait for it to decode it back to english.
